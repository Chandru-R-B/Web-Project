Advantages of E-Agriculture

  • Improved Access to Information
  • Precision Farming
  • Market Access and E-Commerce
  • Financial Inclusion
  • Real-Time Monitoring
  • Data-Driven Decision Making


Disadvantages of E-Agriculture

  • Limited access to technology
  • Implementing e-agriculture technologies can be expensive
  • Farmers need to be trained to effectively use e-agriculture tools
  • production and disposal of electronic devices can contribute to electronic waste
