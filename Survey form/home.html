               E-agriculture, or electronic agriculture, refers to the 
                    use of information and communication technologies (ICTs) in 
                    the agricultural sector. It encompasses a wide range of technologie,
                    including internet-based platforms, mobile applications, sensors, drones, 
                    and other digital tools to enhance various aspects of agriculture. 
                    The goal of e-agriculture is to improve efficiency, productivity, 
                    and sustainability in the agricultural value chain.
